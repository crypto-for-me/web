Why CryptoForMe?

Decentralized

Crypto currencies are decentralized, which allows for people in authoritarian regimes (especially journalists) to pay without the government being able to trace it back easily.

Private

Privacy is a human right. A lot of popular cryptocurrency exchange platforms are closed source and contain a lot of trackers. CryptoForMe, is open source and is much more privacy-friendly because we believe that everyone should be able to pay without being tracked.

Secure

It's not rare for Amazon or PayPal accounts to get hacked. Most crypto currencies, on the other hand, have strong security measures and allow their users to own multiple wallets.
